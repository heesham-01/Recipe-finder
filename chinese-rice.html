Ingredients

  • 4 cups Cooked and cooled rice (Preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetable (Carrot, peas, corn)
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 1 cup cooked & diced chicken (or shrimp or beef)
  • 2 tablespoons green onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tabespoons oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon seasame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper (or black pepper)
  • 2 tablespoon cooking oil (vegetable peanut oil)

Cooking Instruction

1

Prepare the ingredients

Chop the veggies, mince the garlic, and beat the eggs. Ensure the rice is cold and loosened to prevent clumping.

1

Scramble the eggs

Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat. Add the beaten eggs, scramble until fully cooked, then remove from the pan and set aside.

3

Sauté the aromatics

In the same pan, add the remaining 1 tablespoon oil. Sauté garlic and onions until fragrant (about 1 minute).

4

Cook the protein & veggies

Add the cooked chicken (or protein of choice) and stir-fry for 2 minutes. Then, add the mixed vegetables and stir for another 2 minutes.

5

Add the rice

Increase the heat to high and add the cooked rice. Stir well to combine with the other ingredients, breaking up any clumps.

6

Season the rice

Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), white pepper, and sesame oil. Stir everything thoroughly to coat the rice evenly.

7

Re-add eggs and finish

Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix well. Cook for another minute.

8

Garnish & serve

Turn off the heat, sprinkle with chopped green onions, and serve hot!
